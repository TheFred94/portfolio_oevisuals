About me

I love the visual part of communication. Being able to create a visual identity and communicate with people through digital solutions is what it is about for me. From the simple idea all the way through research, prototyping, testing, implementing and developing and in the end maintaining. Having the possibility to design and develop custom soltions is something I take pride in.

Through my career I have tried a little bit of everything. I have managed both physical and e-com stores and know a thing or two about taking responsibility and facing problems head on. I'm driven and I always try to go the extra mile to get the best result in the end

I like being creative with the solutions I design and i'm able to take that and create some one of a kind designs. It's something I use both privately and professionally

I began coding when I started the Multimediadesign education on KEA in 2022 and instantly fell in love. I guess you can say that I found a silent passion I wasn't aware of. This portfolio site was not something I expected to be able to do a year into the education.
